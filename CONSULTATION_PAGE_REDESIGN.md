**Goal:** Create a high-converting, visually stunning consultation booking experience

## 🎯 Design Philosophy

The new consultation page follows conversion optimization best practices:
1. **Visual hierarchy** - Clear progression from hero → form/calendar → benefits
2. **Reduced friction** - Better organized form with visual grouping
3. **Trust signals** - Stats, benefits, and social proof throughout
4. **Dual conversion paths** - Form OR Calendly (user choice)
5. **Mobile-first** - Sticky calendar on desktop, stacked on mobile

---

## 💡 Pro Tips for Maximum Conversions

### Email Configuration:
When you receive a consultation request, your confirmation email should:
- ✅ Confirm receipt within 5 minutes (automated)
- ✅ Set expectations (24-hour response)
- ✅ Include Emad's photo and signature
- ✅ Provide direct email/phone as alternative
- ✅ Link to case studies or pricing page

### Calendly Setup:
Configure your Calendly event with:
- **Event name:** "Free 20-Minute Consultation"
- **Description:** Mention you'll discuss goals, review options, and provide custom plan
- **Questions:** Keep it minimal (Calendly asks for basics)
- **Reminder emails:** 24 hours + 1 hour before
- **Thank you page:** Redirect to `/thank-you` (already built)

### Follow-Up Sequence:
Day 0: Automated thank you email
Day 1: Personal response from Emad with initial thoughts
Day 2-3: Schedule discovery call
Day 4-5: Send custom proposal after call
Day 7: Follow up if no response
Day 14: Final check-in, offer to answer questions
